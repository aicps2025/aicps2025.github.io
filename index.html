About AICPS'25

About The Event

AICPS'2025 provides an ideal platform for the presentation, discussion, critique, and exchange of innovative ideas among researchers, innovators, inventors, and industrial practitioners concerning the current challenges in the application of Cyber-Physical Systems (CPS) and Artificial Intelligence (AI). This first edition of AICPS will be held in hybrid mode, offering both in-person and virtual participation.

CFP

AICPS'2025 aims to bring together researchers about Artificial Intelligence and Cyber Physical Systems. Junior and senior researchers are invited to submit their original unpublished papers on the topics of the Conference :

  • IoT Ecosystem: From Devices to Edge and Cloud Integration

    Explores the interconnectedness of IoT devices, edge computing, and cloud platforms, focusing on data flow, security, and scalability.

  • Advanced Sensors and Sensing Technologies

    Covers cutting-edge sensor technologies, including their design, fabrication, and applications in various fields, such as healthcare, environment, and industry.

  • Innovative Circuits and Systems for AI and IoT Applications

    Discusses the development of efficient and low-power circuits and systems to support AI and IoT applications, including hardware accelerators and embedded systems.

  • Next-Generation Communications, Connectivity, and Networking

    Explores emerging technologies like 5G, 6G, and beyond, focusing on high-speed data transmission, low-latency communication, and network security.

  • Big Data, Data Engineering, and Advanced Analytics

    Covers techniques for processing, analyzing, and extracting insights from large and complex datasets, including data mining, machine learning, and data visualization.

  • Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Cognitive Computing

    Explores the development and application of AI techniques, such as deep learning, natural language processing, and computer vision, to solve complex problems.

  • Blockchain and Distributed Ledger Technologies

    Discusses the principles, applications, and challenges of blockchain technology, including its use in finance, supply chain management, and healthcare.

  • Design Automation in the Era of AI & IoT: From Silicon to Systems

    Explores the use of AI and automation tools to design complex integrated circuits and systems, accelerating the development of innovative products.

  • Cybersecurity and Privacy Solutions

    Covers the latest techniques and strategies for protecting digital systems and data from cyber threats, including encryption, intrusion detection, and privacy-preserving technologies.

  • Intelligent Robotics and Autonomous Systems

    Explores the development of intelligent robots and autonomous systems, including their applications in manufacturing, healthcare, and transportation.

  • Emerging Applications, Processes, and Digital Services

    Discusses innovative applications and services enabled by emerging technologies, such as the Internet of Things, artificial intelligence, and blockchain.

  • Advanced Computer Architectures and Embedded Systems

    Explores the design and optimization of computer architectures and embedded systems for high-performance and energy-efficient computing.

  • Bio-Informatics

    Covers the application of computational techniques to analyze and interpret biological data, including genomics, proteomics, and bioinformatics.

Mascara City

Mascara city location info and gallery

Mascara, Algeria

Mascara Province is located in western Algeria, approximately 380 km from the capital. It covers an area of 5,135 square kilometers, making it a medium-sized province in Algeria. It is bordered by Tlemcen Province to the west, Sidi Bel Abbes Province to the north, Oran and Mostaganem Provinces to the north, and Saida Province to the south.
